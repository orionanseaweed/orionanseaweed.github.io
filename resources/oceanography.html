Giants of the field

  • Dr. Ruth Gates.
    Her research was dedicated to understanding coral reef ecosystems, specifically coral-algal symbiosis and the capacity for corals to acclimatize under future climate change conditions.
  • Prof. James Estes with "Trophic cascades" and Prof. Robert T. Paine with his breakthrough work "Food Web Complexity and Species Diversity".
    They are the pioneers discovering about the organization of marine communities, much of their research on keystone species concept.
