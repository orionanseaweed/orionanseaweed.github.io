Physics List

Early in my university journey, I primarily learned from and worked with physicists and Physics students — the very people who first shaped my critical thinking and offered me a broad, multidisciplinary view of science. Although this might seem unusual for a typical Biology student, from another perspective, it has made my path more unique. That's why this little space was created — as a small tribute to their influence. P/s: One of my amazing PIs is a physicist! ^^
