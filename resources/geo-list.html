Geology List

Creator Pages

  • Dr. Rachel Phillips (aka GeoGirl) is no doubt my absolute favorite geology content creator. The first time I came across her channel was out of curiosity about the explanation of evidence of dark oxygen production at the abyssal seafloor, which then became one of my favorite videos from her. She also hosts an useful website sharing free educational videos and courses over a range of geoscience topics.
  • StrucGeology is a channel about Structural Geology, Neotectonic and Geohazard.
  • Lunar and Planetary Institute is another good choice for enthusiasts to access many presentation videos and reports on Planetary Sciences.

Communities

  • Geology Discord Server.

Textbooks

  • Essentials of Geology (Stephen Marshak, 2022)
