About me

Hi, I am Hoang Xuan Tung, you can call me Kane. I am a science enthusiast. I love biology, astrobiology, geology and beyond.

I'm an undergraduate majoring in Biological engineering at the School of Chemistry and Life Sciences[url], Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Vietnam. Throughout my journey, I've been fortunate to be surrounded by inspiring mentors and talented peers, which has fueled my deep passion for various scientific fields, especially microbiology and astrobiology.

Research & Vision

My research interests focus on the identification and preservation of microbial strains, microbial diversity, metagenomics, genome breeding, and systems metabolic engineering. I am also interested in applying biological approaches to tackle questions at the intersection of biology and other disciplines, including astrobiology, biophysics, environmental biology, evolutionary biology, and geomicrobiology.

Miscellaneous

In my spare time, I often immerse myself in books and musics, play badminton, or go trekking.

I also sometimes play video games, one of my most memorable moments was winning the gold medal in Arena of Valor category at Elympics 2023 (FOC-VNNTU) with my amazing teammates.
