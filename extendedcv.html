Jul. 2022 — now

Head Librarian at D Free Book

Dec. 2023 — Nov. 2024
  • As a member, I contributed to the operation and maintained the gentle beauty of DFB, assisted with book organization and borrower support while helping to promote reading and foster trust within the community.
  • As a leader,I led a team of 15 members in managing the library's collection and data entry, as well as coordinating the circulation of over 1,000 donated books (excluding event sources) to other organizations, schools and libraries across the country (e.g. here and here). During my term, we successfully managed over 6,322 book loans, 3,168 book borrowers and welcomed 814 first-time readers, leveraging IF(NA), UNIQUE, COUNTIF and XLOOKUP functions of Excel for efficient data management.
  • We also collaborated with external relations and events departments to organize key library activities, including "Exchange Books for Trees", "Exchange Books for Flowers", "D Free Fair: Summer Garden", "Bookshelves for Children", and the "Drawing Stories" writing contest. These events attracted significant participation, helped raise funds for the library and resulted in the collection of over 1,070 books and the distribution of 2,907 books to primary and secondary school children in the highlands.
